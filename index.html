Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of healthy food items do you sell?

We offer a variety of organic fruits, whole grains, nuts, seeds, natural sweeteners, gluten-free options, and other nutrient-rich foods.

Are your products organic or preservative-free?

Yes, most of our items are organic, free from harmful chemicals, and do not contain artificial preservatives.

How can I be sure your food items are fresh?

We source directly from trusted farms and suppliers. Our products are stored carefully and delivered in safe, hygienic packaging to maintain freshness.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free products?

Yes, we provide a wide range of vegan-friendly and gluten-free options to suit different dietary needs

How long does delivery usually take?

Orders are processed quickly and delivered within 2–5 business days, depending on your location.

What if I receive a damaged or expired product?

If this happens, you can request a return or exchange within 7 days, and we’ll make sure you get a replacement or refund.
